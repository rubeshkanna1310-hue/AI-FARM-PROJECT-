My paddy leaves are turning brown at the tips. What is wrong?
🌾 KisanAIBrown leaf tips on paddy are often Bacterial Leaf Blight (BLB). Check if the yellowing starts from leaf tips and moves inward. Apply Copper Oxychloride 50% WP at 3g/litre. Avoid excess nitrogen fertilizer.
Which fertilizer for better yield in black cotton soil?
🌾 KisanAIBlack cotton soil (Vertisol) is rich in calcium but often deficient in Nitrogen & Phosphorus. Apply DAP (Di-ammonium Phosphate) 50 kg/acre at sowing + Urea 30 kg/acre in split doses. Add Zinc Sulphate 10 kg/acre once in 3 seasons.

Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Is KisanAI free to use?+
Yes, KisanAI is completely free for all farmers. There are no hidden charges, subscriptions, or login requirements. Just open the website and start asking questions.
Can I ask questions in Hindi or regional languages?+
Yes! KisanAI understands Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati and more. Type naturally in any language and you'll get a clear, helpful response.
How accurate is the disease diagnosis?+
KisanAI is trained on agricultural science literature and expert knowledge. For common diseases it is highly accurate. For serious outbreaks, we always recommend also consulting your local Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) or district agriculture officer.
Does it work without internet on a basic phone?+
Currently KisanAI requires an internet connection. We recommend using it when you have Wi-Fi or mobile data. The site is lightweight and works well even on 2G/3G connections.
Are the market prices updated daily?+
The market price section shows indicative prices based on recent mandi data. For exact local prices, we always recommend checking your nearest APMC mandi or the eNAM portal for the most current rates.
Can I get help applying for PM-KISAN or crop insurance?+
Yes! The Government Schemes section explains all major schemes with eligibility, benefits, and how to apply. You can also ask the AI directly for step-by-step guidance on any scheme.

Agricultural Schemes & Subsidies

Complete information on major central and state government schemes for farmers. Click any scheme to ask the AI how to apply.

🌾
PM-KISAN
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi — direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmer families in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each.

₹6,000/year in 3 installments of ₹2,000
All landholding farmer families eligible
Direct bank transfer — no middlemen
Register at pmkisan.gov.in or CSC
🛡️
PMFBY — Crop Insurance
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Comprehensive crop insurance covering pre-sowing, standing crop, post-harvest, and localized risks. Very low premium rates for farmers — government pays the bulk.

Kharif: 2% premium; Rabi: 1.5% premium
Horticulture crops: max 5% premium
Coverage for drought, flood, pest, disease
Apply through bank or CSC before cutoff
💳
Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
NABARD / All nationalized banks

Flexible credit facility for farmers to meet crop cultivation expenses, post-harvest expenses, and allied agricultural activities at subsidized interest rates.

Credit up to ₹3 lakh at 7% interest
Effective rate 4% with govt interest subvention
Applicable for fisheries and animal husbandry too
Apply at nearest bank branch with land documents
🧪
Soil Health Card
Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare

Free soil testing and health card issued every 2 years showing soil nutrient status and fertilizer recommendations for 12 parameters.

Free soil testing at government labs
12 nutrient parameters tested
Tailored fertilizer recommendation
Apply at Krishi Vigyan Kendra or tehsil office
💧
PM Krishi Sinchayee Yojana
Ministry of Jal Shakti

"Har Khet Ko Pani, More Crop Per Drop" — scheme to expand irrigated area and improve water use efficiency through micro-irrigation subsidies.

55% subsidy on drip irrigation for small farmers
45% subsidy for large farmers on micro-irrigation
Additional 10% state top-up in many states
Apply via state agriculture department
🏪
eNAM — Digital Market
Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium

National Agriculture Market — online trading platform connecting farmers directly with buyers across India, enabling better price discovery and eliminating middlemen.

Sell produce online to buyers across India
Real-time price discovery and bidding
Linked to 1,260+ APMC mandis
Register with Aadhaar + bank account
Our mission

About KisanAI

🌾

KisanAI was built with one mission: to give every Indian farmer — regardless of education level, smartphone access, or economic status — the same quality of agricultural advice that large farm owners get from expensive consultants.

India has 140 million farm households. Most farming decisions are still made on guesswork, traditional knowledge, or advice from fertilizer shop owners with commercial interests. Bad advice means crop loss, debt, and despair.

We built an AI system trained on decades of ICAR research, agricultural university publications, and expert agronomist knowledge — and put it in a simple chat interface anyone can use on any phone.

Our system supports 8+ languages, covers 50+ crops, 200+ diseases and pests, and is available 24 hours a day — even at 3am when a farmer notices something wrong with his crop.
